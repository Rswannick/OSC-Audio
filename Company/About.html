about us 

We are an independent audio company bringing studio quality, affordable plugins to everyone. Our goal is to help you make music that sounds great by designing the tools you need to create music that truly moves you.

As a small and dedicated team, we firmly believe that making music should be an enjoyable and fulfilling experience for all. That's why we're here, working tirelessly to bring you high-quality plugins that won't break the bank. Our plugins are designed with user-friendliness in mind, while incorporating innovative features to ignite your creative spark. We're also excited to share that we have plans to release our apps for iOS, so you can make music wherever inspiration strikes. Keep an eye out for upcoming announcements!

We want to express our appreciation to each and every one of you for being a part of our community. Your support and feedback are invaluable to us. Check out the latest downloads in our Gumroad shop and explore the range of plugins we offer.

Stay tuned for more updates on our upcoming releases for iOS. Thank you for joining us on this musical journey!

Dusty Rings Keys is a creative lo-fi piano created with a powerful physical modeling module, the Mutable Instruments® Rings, run through the Marantz® PMD221, tape cassette for vintage grit and hiss.

Designed with infinite sustain in mind, samples are perfectly looped and ready for use in Decent Sampler or the sampler of your choice.

Full Key range of A-1 through C7, with included Chorus and Reverb effects with DAW automation available for expressive control.

plateVerb is a macOS, Windows, and now iOS compatible stereo plate reverb. Designed for VST3, AU, AUv3, and stand-alone use.

Packed with 7 plate modes to give you a wide range of plate sounds and bring a shimmering, smooth, beautiful ambience to any of your sources.

Unlock the full sonic potential of each mode with in-depth controls; Mix, Dampening, Ducking, Left/Right Repeats and Decay. Their three band EQ Section (Low, Mid, and High), and master controls (Master Volume & Pan).

All settings can be saved and loaded in your sessions as well as crossplatform presets. All parameters offer automation control to get the most expressive sound. 

With 20 included presets and a revamped preset section, you'll be able to dial in any space with ease.

specifications
  • macOS 10.14 or later

  • Windows 10 or later

  • iOS 15 or later

  • Formats: Stand-alone, VST2, VST3, AU, AUv3
